# Todos

## Creating Todos

Type in the input field at the top and press **Enter** or click **Add**. New todos appear at the bottom of the list.

## Sub-Todos

Click the **Add sub-todo** option from the `...` menu or right-click context menu on any todo. Sub-todos are indented below their parent and can be expanded/collapsed with the chevron icon.

Deleting a parent todo also deletes all its sub-todos.

## Completing Todos

Click the checkbox to toggle completion. Completed todos show with a strikethrough. You can always toggle your own todos. Toggling others' requires the `complete_any_todo` permission.

## Inline Editing

Click **Edit** from the `...` menu or context menu. The title becomes an input field. Press **Enter** to save or **Escape** to cancel.

## Reordering

On desktop, drag todos using the grip handle on the left to reorder. The new order is saved automatically via the reorder API. Drag-and-drop is disabled on mobile.

## Comments

Click the **Comments** button (or the comment count badge) to open the comments dialog. Comments are threaded per-todo, showing username, timestamp, and body.

## Multi-Selection

Right-click a todo and choose **Select**, or use the `...` menu. Once in selection mode:

- Click items to toggle selection
- **Shift+click** for range selection
- A selection bar appears with bulk actions: Select All, Mark Complete, Mark Incomplete, Delete, Clear

## Context Menu

Right-click any todo to open a context menu with: Add sub-todo, Comments, Select/Deselect, Select All, Edit, Mark complete/incomplete, Delete. Available actions depend on your permissions.
